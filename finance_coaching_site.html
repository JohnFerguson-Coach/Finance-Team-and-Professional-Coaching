What I Do

I provide one-on-one and group coaching for finance professionals and teams. Whether you’re a finance leader seeking to inspire your department, or a finance assistant wanting to unlock hidden talent, my coaching is designed to build clarity, resilience, and high performance in a fast-changing business environment.

About Your Coach

John Ferguson, an ex CFO and FD, brings over 25 years of experience in finance leadership, including senior roles in corporate finance, FP&A, transformation and business partnering. He previously worked in both the public and private sector, in companies that are privately owned to large multi-nationals. He is a trained professional coach with a track record of developing high-performing teams and guiding individuals to achieve career breakthroughs.

John combines deep understanding of finance culture, environment, challenges and issues with coaching methodologies rooted in emotional intelligence, strategic thinking, and behavioural change. His approach is practical, empathetic, and results-driven, enabling finance professionals to excel in both personal and leadership capacities. John is an associate of the Chartered Institute of Management Accountants, a Chartered Global Management Accountant member, a Fellow of the Royal Geographical Society and in his spare time, follows Liverpool FC, loves travelling and enjoys cooking.
